What is a Community-supported Bakery?

Subcriptions

Help us with the dough!

From January onwards, there will be the possibility for customers to become members, and to subscribe to our 'bread share' scheme. The idea is to pay for your weekly bread in advance for a year or six months. This helps us a lot, as we know that there will be a steady demand for the future, and we are able to purchase ingredients and make our bread with less worry about waste or shortages. For you, it means that you will be getting bread every week, and do not have to worry about ordering. In addition to that, you will become a member or the bakery, and will be invited to general assemblies. The exact terms will come soon
