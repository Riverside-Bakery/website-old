What is a Community-supported Bakery?

Getting our bread:

Weekly order

To be able to order your bread on a weekly basis, subscribe to our weekly newsletter it will keep you up to date on what is available.

The process is very simple:

  • Pick up your computer
  • Order your bread (quantity, type, size, collection point)
  • Wait patiently, nibbling on the crumbs from last week,
  • Come to collect your bread at your collection point, a bright smile on your face,
  • Leave money in the box,
  • Tick yourself off the list,
  • Walk, cycle, drive away, resisting the temptation of eating everything on the spot,
  • Enjoy, share, and let us know what you think!
